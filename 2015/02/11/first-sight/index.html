No plus signs standing out in a field and these abstract thigns has not so much relations with our emotions.

do more exercises to bring those ideas into reality for you.
practice makes permanent.

week2 chunk, recall and Einstellung

chunk

chunk helps you unite bits of information together through many neutron firmed together.
可以通过chunk得到main idea
how to form a chunk?
学习的时候通过listen和watch others，先对每个小部分的内容形成mini chunk,然后在将她们联合起来。
points of chunk:

  1. learn bit by bit
  2. work through examples
    • focus on connections between every step as well as understand. *

注意每一个步骤之间的联系比单独某一个步骤更加重要。
basic steps for chunking:

  1. focus your undivided attention on information you want to chunk, with no interruptions.
  2. understand the basic ideals you’ve trying to chunk.
    check if you can solve the problems you think you understand until you can actually do it by yourself.
  3. gain context by practicing, you should know when to sue the solving techniques rather than other techniques.

知道什么时候用什么方法解决一个问题。
拿到资料的时候，看图片和大标题可以形成大致印象，以便把握大块内容之间的联系

recall

After you finished a material, try to see how much you can recall from materials you’ve just read.
Using recall, mental retrieving the key ideas, words and short sentenses to synthesis key ideas is good.
However, 谷歌或阅读笔记会让你产生已经记住这些知识的错觉，思维导图也并不是那么有用。
重复阅读笔记只是浪费时间，最有效的方法是遮住内容回想， test what you’ve learned.
recall the material when you are outside the usual place you study
在不同的场景回想可以增强记忆

week 3 procrastination and deep memory

procrastination

使用意志力will power是十分消耗精神能量的，不应该用来抵抗很大的procrastination，可以把拖延分成小的原因去用will power解决
一般对不开心的事情，人们会产生拖延情绪。
unhappy feelings
|
to put off tasks
|
more painful
打败拖延需要形成习惯，习惯可以节省精神能量，因为它不需要思考
habits的成因:

  1. the cue: just a thing on your to-do list
  2. the routine: that’s the zombie of procrastination
  3. the reward: 尝到好处可以更加愉快地坚持, finding ways to reward good habitats is important to excaping procrastination
  4. belief: 需要相信你自己可以打败拖延
    some tips:
  5. 在开始学习得时候put negative feelings aside.
  6. 当不想开始一个任务时，应该更加注重过程而不是结果.focus on process rather than products
    eg: I’m going to study programming for 25 minutes, rather than I have to finish this program in 25 minutes.

利用你的zombies to help
to overide a habit, you need to change your reaction to a cue
Four parts of habits

  1. the cue. 弄清楚是什么导致了拖延
  2. the routines. Have a plan, develop a new ritual, shifting your reactions
  3. the reward. Habits are powerful because they create neurological cravings.
  4. the belief. Believe you can do it

需要随时judge life and learning

  • weekly list of key tasks 每周列任务清单
  • daily ‘to-do’ list 每天列待办事项
  • plan your quit time 计划好啥时候不学了
  • do things you don’t want to do firstly 最先做你最不愿做的事
  • squeeze time for a break 无论如何挤出时间休息

procrastination summary:

  • keep a plan journal
  • do certain tasks each day
  • get rewards when you finish
  • find procrastination cues
  • trust you can to it
  • have a backup plan
  • do most difficult things first
  • focus on process rather than products

deepen your memory

  1. 图像与右脑记忆相关，use visual memory can encapsulate concepts.
    The funnier and more evocative the images are, the better.
    space learning over time is more effective than mass learning once
  2. create meaning ful groups that simplify the materials
    memory palace 记忆宫殿
    calling into mind a familiar place, the more you use this technique, the more creative you are.
    short-term memory —–consolidation—-> long term memory —- reactionation —-> short term memory

tips of being a better learner:

learn using metaphor and analogy
